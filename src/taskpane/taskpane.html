Step 1

Please load one of the workbooks

Step 2

Load the Productivity Tracker Workbook

Step 3

Update any value on JC Progress Entry sheet on columns: A, B, D, E, P and T. And then click the Save to DB button

Step 4

You can check the data you entered previously. Please click the Load from DB button. A new sheet will be appended to the workbook where you will see the data that you modified in step 3
